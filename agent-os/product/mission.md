# Product Mission

## Problem

Analyzing bank expenses from CSV exports of the Bankin banking aggregator is cumbersome and lacks dedicated tooling. Users need a simple way to understand their spending patterns without manual spreadsheet work.

## Target Users

General consumers who use Bankin as their banking aggregator and want to gain insights into their financial habits through expense analysis.

## Solution

A dedicated expense analysis application that offers:

- **Native Bankin integration**: Direct support for Bankin CSV export format, eliminating manual data transformation
- **Rich visualization**: Charts and graphs for intuitive understanding of spending patterns
- **Reimbursement tracking**: Associate expenses with people and generate summaries for shared expense management
